# Product Context

## Purpose
EDM Atlantic exists to promote and showcase local Electronic Music DJs and Artists in the Atlantic region, creating a centralized platform for the EDM community.

## Problems It Solves
- **Discoverability**: Makes it easy for fans to discover local EDM talent
- **Artist Promotion**: Provides artists with a professional platform to showcase their work
- **Community Building**: Creates a hub for the Atlantic EDM scene
- **Content Organization**: Centralizes music and sets in one accessible location

## User Experience Goals

### For Fans/Visitors
- Browse local EDM artists easily
- Learn about artists through detailed biographies
- Listen to or access music and DJ sets
- Show support by liking/starring favorite content
- Intuitive, fast, and enjoyable browsing experience

### For Artists
- Professional presentation of their brand
- Showcase their bio and story
- Display their music catalog and DJ sets
- Receive engagement through likes/stars

## Key User Flows

1. **Discover Artists**
   - Visit homepage → Browse artist list → Select artist → View profile

2. **Explore Content**
   - View artist → Read bio → Browse music/sets → Like favorites

3. **Show Support**
   - Find content → Click like/star → Visual confirmation

## Design Principles
- Clean and modern aesthetic fitting EDM culture
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast loading and navigation
- Visual hierarchy emphasizing content
- Clear call-to-actions (like/star buttons)
