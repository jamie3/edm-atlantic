# About Sean Keating

Sean Keating is a prominent figure in Halifax's electronic music scene, known for his deep understanding of house and techno music. With years of experience behind the decks, Sean has become a staple at venues across the city.

## Musical Journey

Sean's passion for electronic music began in the early 2000s when he discovered the underground rave scene. What started as a love for dancing quickly evolved into a desire to create and share music with others. He began DJing in 2010 and hasn't looked back since.

## Style & Sound

His sets are characterized by deep, groovy basslines, hypnotic rhythms, and carefully selected tracks that take the audience on a journey. Whether playing intimate club nights or larger festival stages, Sean knows how to read a room and deliver exactly what the dancefloor needs.

## Notable Performances

- Regular resident at Halifax's top electronic music venues
- Played at numerous festivals across the Maritimes
- Has shared the stage with international touring DJs
- Known for his monthly podcast series featuring local and international talent

## Community Impact

Beyond his performances, Sean is dedicated to growing Halifax's electronic music community. He mentors up-and-coming DJs and is actively involved in promoting local events and supporting fellow artists.
