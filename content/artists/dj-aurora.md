# About DJ Aurora

DJ Aurora has been a cornerstone of the Halifax electronic music scene for over 5 years. Known for her dynamic techno and house sets, she brings an infectious energy to every performance.

## Musical Journey

Aurora's journey into electronic music began in her teenage years when she discovered underground techno at local warehouse parties. What started as a curiosity quickly evolved into a passion, and by age 20, she was learning to DJ.

## Style & Sound

Her signature sound blends deep, driving techno basslines with melodic progressive elements and infectious house grooves. She's known for taking crowds on a journey through different tempos and emotions, always maintaining high energy while respecting the flow of the dancefloor.

## Notable Performances

- Resident DJ at The Underground (Halifax)
- Played at Atlantic Bass Festival 2024
- Monthly shows at Reflections Cabaret
- Regular guest at regional venues across Nova Scotia

## Future Projects

Aurora is currently working on her debut EP, set to release in late 2024, featuring collaborations with several Atlantic Canadian producers.
