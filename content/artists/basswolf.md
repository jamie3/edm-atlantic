# About BassWolf

BassWolf is Saint John's premier Drum & Bass artist, known for producing hard-hitting neurofunk and rolling liquid DnB. His productions have been featured on several UK-based labels and played by international DJs.

## Background

Growing up in Saint John, BassWolf was heavily influenced by the British jungle and drum & bass movement of the 90s. He started producing in 2018 and quickly developed a unique sound that blends heavy neurofunk elements with melodic liquid vibes.

## Production Style

His tracks are characterized by complex drum patterns, deep sub-bass, and atmospheric pads. Whether it's a dark neurofunk roller or a melodic liquid anthem, BassWolf's production quality stands out in the Atlantic scene.

## Achievements

- Released on Blackout Music NL
- Supported by Andy C, Noisia, and Friction
- Headlined DnB nights across the Maritimes
- Runs monthly "Bass Culture" event in Saint John

## Collaborations

BassWolf frequently collaborates with other Atlantic producers and has tracks in the works with artists from Toronto and Montreal.
